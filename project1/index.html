Covid Peril
The Medical Association of Jamaica is requesting a review of a Ministry of Health and Wellness protocol. This requires health-care workers to continue working if they test positive for coronavirus but show no symptoms.

Education ministry launches initiative to locate disengaged students
The Ministry of Education and Youth will implement its ‘Yard to Yard Find the Child’ Initiative. This initiative is credited as a national call to identify, locate, and re-engage students with their schooling. Youth workers as well as social workers will complement school based teams in order to help with this course of action to help students continue their education.
