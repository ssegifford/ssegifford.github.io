Watersport




Jamaica provides some of the best experiences one can have on/in water. If you're not afraid of getting your hair wet and actually welcome it there's no shortage of activities available.

Get up close and personal with the world in the sea by scuba diving and/or snorkeling. Or take a cool ride on a glass bottom boat and see the world under the water while remaining above it. And if you crave more of a rush, grab a jetski and enjoy a ride on a beautiful sunny day.

If everything you've read so far doesn't already have you on a plane, just imagine you and a group of family/friends on a big boat enjoying a private party on the ocean while enjoying the view of the sunset. Are you on a plane yet??
