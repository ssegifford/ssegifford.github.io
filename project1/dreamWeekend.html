Dream Weekend




DREAM WEEKEND is Jamaica's most anticipated celebration of the year. It's a week of nonstop partying along the beautiful coast of Negril, a 7-mile stretch of one of the world's most beautiful beaches in the world. During this week Jamaica celebrates their independence from the British colony on August 6, 1962. For anyone who loves to party this is an experience like no other.
Party ALL DAY... AND ALL NIGHT.

For 5 days straight thousands of people partake in different parties, one after the other. There are pool parties, beach parties, and club parties for everyone to enjoy. Purchase a wrist band and you'll get access to every single one and have the experience of a lifetime.
